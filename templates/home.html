Well Production Management

Oil and gas is a multi-billion dollar industry with many competitors across many basins across the US. The industry generates over $150 billion/year in revenue spread over 25 producing basins in the lower 48 states alone. With over 20,000 wells drilled in the US every year, the Well Production Management system aims to allow users to track oil production of wells drilled by companies across multiple basins and various formations. Understanding the performance of existing wells will allow for operators to make more informed decisions regarding their development strategies. Our implementation is meant to be a simplified model of how a database like this works. In the real world these databases can store over 100,000 wells in a single basin alone. What we will do is build a database that could store 1000 wells over a handful of basins and formations to simulate a relational database that tracks oil production of multiple companies.
